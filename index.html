Airbnb's of New York

Why stay in an Airbnb while in new york?

Staying in one of the most popular places in the world can be a very expensive endevour. Hotels and apartments for rent from traditional source can cost thousands of dollars a night and the value for money can be very. Airbnb presents the unique opitunity to stay with a local new york resident. Not only do you benifit from avoiding the busyness of a regular hotel, but you gain the valuable knowledge only a new york resident could have.

There are 4924 different accomidation options availble through this service. Of that number you can choose whether you would like an entire apartment , a private room or even a shared room with the owner. Individual prices range from 1200 to 50 dollars a night. In this first map all stays are shown and their respective price per night are shown though the shade of red they are displayed by.

Price

Price may be the greatest factor when planning a stay away from home. These two bar charts show the median price for both the cost to rent per night and the service fee. If you were on a tight budget you would be looking to rent a private room in Bronx due to its median price of $534 dollars a night. Watch out for shared rooms in Brooklyn as they have a median service fee of $169 dollars

Reviews

When planning a place to stay, reviews from previous guest can be pivotal in narrowing down your choices. This scatterplot shows the average rating of each location spread by volume of reviews and the date of the last review. The number of reviews is logorythmically scaled to provide a more normalised respresentation of the availible data.

The graph can be filtered by score and date to ensure that you can find a location with a recent and high rating reviews

Points of Interest

New York is a terminal of famous attractions, planning your stay to be near points of interest you want to visit can avoid travel costs and save you valuable time that you could be relaxing! This map shows the Statue of Liberty, the Brooklyn Bridge, the Empire State Building, Times Square, Central Park and the Muesem of New York. The black lines connect to airbnb locations within the specified range and the shade of blue for each dot represents the total number of points of interests in range of the stay.
